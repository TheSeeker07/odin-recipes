Lasagna

Description

This recipe covers one of my favorite dishes known as Lasagna. It can take some time but the end result will be a dish that you can share with friends and family around the dinner table. Alternatively, you can be rightfully selfish and cook it for yourself! No shame either way, the most important thing is that you enjoy cooking the dish and thoroughly enjoy the finished meal.

Ingredients

  • Meat: Sweet Italian Sausage and Lean Ground Beef
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products: Can of Crush Tomatoes, Two Cans of Tomato Sauce, and Two Cans of Tomato Paste
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings: Fresh Parsley, Dried Basil Leaves, Salt, Italian Seasoning, Fennel Seeds, and Black Pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, Mozzarella, and Ricotta Cheese
  • Single Egg
