Chicken Thighs

Description

This is a recipe for chicken thighs cooked within an air fryer. It is hard to mess up, easy to clean, and relatively healthy compared to the fried variation. Follow along below and enjoy chicken that won't break the bank.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Olive Oil
  • Spices and Seasonings: Salt, Pepper, Paprika, and Garlic Powder
