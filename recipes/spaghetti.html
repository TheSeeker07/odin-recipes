Spaghetti

Description

A famous Italian dish that is known around the world. Spaghetti is an easy recipe that will have you coming back for seconds. Garlic bread is not optional but also not explained here. Maybe later.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Medium Onion
  • Garlic Cloves
  • Green Bell Pepper
  • Can of Diced Tomatoes
  • Can of Tomato Sauce
  • Can of Tomato Paste
  • Dried Oregano
  • Dried Basil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Combine ground beef, onion, garlic, and green pepper in a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Cook and stir until meat is browned and vegetables are tender.
  3. Stir diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste into the pan. Season with oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer spaghetti sauce for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  4. Serve!
